Patient Consent to use Tali AI (Short Version) Our clinic uses a software tool called Tali to create notes for your medical record. Using Tali helps your doctor focus more on talking to you and caring for you, instead of typing at the computer.

Tali records what you and your doctor say in an appointment, and uses AI to write a summary for your doctor to check. Your doctor can then edit it if they want, and put it in your medical record.

Tali does not have any other information about you. Tali does not keep the recording. Tali encrypts what they store very carefully to protect your privacy. Tali will not use any of your personal health information, or the recording of the appointment, to train their AI.

There is more detailed information available about how Tali handles information that you can read. You can also ask your doctor or clinic staff questions about Tali and how it is used in this clinic.
