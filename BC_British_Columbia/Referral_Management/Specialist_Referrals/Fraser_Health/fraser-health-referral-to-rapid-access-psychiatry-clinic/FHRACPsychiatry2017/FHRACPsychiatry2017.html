WHO CAN REFER TO THE RAPID ACCESS CLINIC?

Family Physicians and Nurse Practitioners

WHO IS NOT ELIGIBLE FOR THE RAPID ACCESS CLINIC?
  • Clients presenting with imminent/high risk of harm
  • Children & Youth < 19 years old
  • Clients who do not reside within the community of the Mental Health Centre
  • Have been previously assessed by the RAC clinic or other psychiatrist in the past 6 months
  • GP/client wanting a Second Opinion
  • Forensic Service clients
  • Self-referrals by clients or families
  • Clients with chronic/serious and persistent Mental Illness
  • IQ Impression <70
