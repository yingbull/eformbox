Inclusion Criteria:
  • Any adult patient above age of 17 years old that need urgent optimization of an active Internal Medicine related issue on an outpatient basis
Exclusion Criteria:
  • Patient’s age of less than 17
  • Addiction related referral
  • Primary Care related referral
Inclusion Criteria:
  • IV diuresis that need adjustment of diuretics and transition to PO diuretics
  • Congestive Heart Failure who need titration of cardio protective medications and who are unsafe to be discharged prior to achieving optimal medical therapy
  • Refractory hypertension
  • Refractory diabetes who need adjustment of Insulin
  • IV antibiotics
  • COPD and Asthma exacerbation who need immediate follow up
  • Acute renal injury who need volume assessment and adjustment of their medications on a daily basis
  • Complex autoimmune disorders
  • Cancer patients who are sent for admission to facilitate the delineation of unknown malignancies or autoimmune diseases
  • Fever not yet diagnosed but not septic
  • Afib/flutter for rate control
  • DVT/PE for short term follow up
  • Community acquire pneumonia
  • Post syncope workup
  • POST STEMI echo follow up
Exclusion Criteria:
  • Require Oxygen therapy (unless on home oxygen)
  • Medically unstable
  • Lack appropriate social support with transportation/drive to the clinic or have no phone to contact.
  • Cognitive impairment and unable to follow instructions
  • Renal Replacement therapy
  • Chronic pain or addiction as the main concern or in need of titration of opioids/narcotics
