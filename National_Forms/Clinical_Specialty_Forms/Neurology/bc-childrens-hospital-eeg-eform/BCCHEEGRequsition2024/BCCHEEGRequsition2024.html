Sleep Deprivation Instructions

Age under 1 year - Go to bed normally - stay awake for 3 hours before the test. Total sleep 3 hours less than normal

Age 1-3 years - Go to bed 2 hours later - Wake up 2 hours later. Total sleep 4 hours less than normal

Age over 3 years - Go to bed at midnight (12 am) - Wake up 5 am. Total sleep 5 hours less than normal
